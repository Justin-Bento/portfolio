To Re-teach mysellf the basics of website development. I decide check out [cssbattle.dev](cssbattle.dev) to help me figure out where I am with CSS, and use freecodecamp's responsive web design course and their front-end development libraries to help me learn how to code.

## Sketching Out The Rules For The Battle

To challange myself I looked at Challanges On youtube to help me get a genral idea on how to use the website.

1. Limit myself to one battle each day to help me staying engaged and motivated.

2. Only refrence documentation 3 times when I am stuck.

3. Set A time limit for 25-minutes, to solve the problem and minify the css.
