The name of the company is Forcon and it stands for Foregin Exchange. Our mission is to teach foreign students how to transition into Canada. By showing them Canada's quirks, culture, laws and work environment through an online platform. So students can adapt quickly into a new country.

You can [Click Here](https://youtu.be/Sv-btZM28LQ) to watch our launch video but keep in mind it was made by students that have zero editing and videography experience. Please watch at your own risk.

## Launching Forcon

The first stages of developing a brand uses the lean startup method. We also looked at other pitch videos for inspiiration and see how comapnies got started. When defining Forcons brand’s voice we wrote positioning statement from the lean startup template.

We then set up a meeting to brainstorm ideas and get a general consensus on understanding the purpose for the comapny. Help internatioinal studnets seemed to revolve around that meetinig and we wanted to make their college and university life easier, by

- Helping designers to get involved into the open source community,
- Host Reusme Templates and CV for people to purchase
- Help international

Then we booked a meeting to have a peer review session to share what stundets are professors and other students
